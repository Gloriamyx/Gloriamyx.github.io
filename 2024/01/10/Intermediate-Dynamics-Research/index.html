Intermediate Dynamics Research

Author: Yuxin Ma Date: January 10, 2024  15:47:16

Since December 2022, I have been a research assistant under the guidance of Professor Dennis Bernstein, who is also my instructor for the ME540 course.

My primary responsibilities involve contributing to the revision and development of new content for his dynamic book. Additionally, I have derived equations of motion for various examples using both Euler-Newton Dynamics and Lagrangian Dynamics.

I am now doing some research about Predictive Cost Adaptive Control.

I have successfully solved more than 100 dynamics problems.

Two manuscripts are in preparation.
