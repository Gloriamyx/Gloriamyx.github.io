About Me

Greetings,

Welcome to my website!

I am Yuxin Ma, currently pursuing a master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Michigan. You can view my CV by clicking here.

If you are interested in my research, you can explore my project homepage, which highlights some of my diverse research experiences. My work spans a wide array of fields, contributing to a comprehensive skill set that includes dynamics, control, machine learning, computer vision, motion planning, data processing and analysis, mechanical design, fabrication, and more. This diversity reflects my inherently curious personality.

Aside from the mentioned experiences, I have also worked on smaller course projects and contributed to projects where my responsibilities were limited to specific parts. These encompass projects such as **implementing voice navigation for Gazebo robots, automating the recognition and retrieval of cans by robots, utilizing eye-tracking technology for robot manipulation, employing Predictive Cost Adaptive Control for online system identification, controlling a simulated robot arm through Principal Component Analysis of IMU Data, conducting a comparison of Nonlinear dimensionality reduction methods, and more. **Feel free to reach out if you’re interested in obtaining more details.

The continual exploration of new territories has transformed my mindset, instilling a fearlessness towards challenges and a determination to acquire new knowledge. Exposure to algorithms from different fields has enhanced my adaptability in problem-solving. Moreover, it also has led me to recognize intriguing similarities between algorithms. While I have immensely enjoyed my past research experiences, they are too short to create something. Looking ahead, I aspire to delve deeply into a specific direction, striving to achieve meaningful and remarkable results.

During my leisure time, I find joy in engaging in handicrafts and immersing myself in novels. Fueled by a strong sense of curiosity, I constantly explore new activities and enthusiastically share my discoveries with friends. Volunteer work holds a special place in my heart, and I have a history of organizing and participating in various initiatives since high school. If there are any engaging or meaningful activities, please don’t hesitate to reach out to me.

Thank you so much for visiting my website! Your presence is truly valued. If you have any feedback or questions, I would love to hear from you. Feel free to explore and reach out – your support means a lot!
